# Sinapso Strategy

## Target problem

Independent knowledge operators repeatedly reconstruct context before important research and decisions, yet still miss relevant knowledge they already possess. Generic AI lacks their accumulated history, so its answers are shallow and useful work disappears into disposable conversations.

## Our approach

Sinapso becomes a trusted knowledge partner grounded in the user's entire, user-owned knowledge world. The local platform remains free and open; optional paid services provide secure continuity across devices without turning cloud storage or a vendor format into the source of truth.

## Who it's for

**Primary:** Independent knowledge operators - Consultants, researchers, founders, analysts, and other people who maintain substantial personal knowledge and need to turn it into research, decisions, and durable deliverables.

## Key metrics

- **Weekly value sessions** - Weekly grounded conversations, research, or decision workflows that create or materially improve a durable artifact.
- **Four-week retention** - Activated users still completing value sessions four weeks later.
- **Managed-service conversion** - Active local users who subscribe to secure sync, backup, and mobile continuity.
- **Paid retention** - Subscribers retained, excluding refunds and involuntary payment failures.
- **Referred-user activation** - Shared-document recipients who register and complete their first value session.

## Tracks

### Knowledge partner experience

Grounded conversation, research, synthesis, and decision support that produces durable work.

_Why it serves the approach:_ The product must help knowledge act, not merely store or display it.

### Open local platform

Markdown/YAML interoperability, ingestion, visualization, and extensibility independent of Obsidian or any vendor.

_Why it serves the approach:_ Users can trust the partner because their files remain portable, inspectable, and under their control.

### Secure knowledge continuity

Private synchronization, recoverable backups, versioning, and mobile access.

_Why it serves the approach:_ A partner grounded in the user's whole world must remain available without making the cloud the canonical owner.

### Share-to-growth loop

Selective document sharing that delivers immediate value to recipients and invites them into their own Sinapso workspace.

_Why it serves the approach:_ Useful knowledge becomes the acquisition channel instead of paid reach alone.

## Not working on

- Full real-time multi-user collaboration, presence, and complex permission matrices before document sharing proves demand.
- Enterprise administration, SSO, and procurement workflows before the individual paid loop works.
- Full mobile 3D parity before the companion experience proves retention.

## Marketing

**One-liner:** Your whole knowledge world, ready to talk, research, and decide with you.

**Key message:** Sinapso is a free, open local knowledge platform for files you own. Optional managed services keep that world privately synchronized, recoverable, and available from mobile.
