# Sinapso Domain Context

Canonical product language. This file is a glossary only; strategy, requirements, implementation details, and status belong elsewhere.

## Language

**Independent knowledge operator**:
A person who maintains substantial personal or professional knowledge and repeatedly turns it into research, decisions, or durable deliverables.
_Avoid_: knowledge worker when referring to the initial target user; it is too broad.

**Knowledge world**:
The complete user-owned collection of notes, sources, links, metadata, and durable outputs that Sinapso can navigate and ground work against.
_Avoid_: vault when the statement is not specific to an Obsidian vault.

**Local core**:
The free, open product capabilities that run against user-owned files without requiring a Sinapso account or managed continuity subscription.
_Avoid_: free tier; it implies an intentionally restricted subscription tier.

**Value session**:
A grounded conversation, research, synthesis, or decision workflow that creates or materially improves a durable artifact.
_Avoid_: session when no durable user value was produced.

**Durable artifact**:
A user-owned output preserved in the knowledge world, such as a note, synthesis, decision record, or research document.
_Avoid_: response or chat when the result is intended to remain useful.

**Managed continuity**:
Optional paid synchronization, backup, recovery, versioning, and mobile availability that preserves local user-owned files as canonical.
_Avoid_: cloud storage; storage alone does not describe the product value or ownership boundary.

**Knowledge partner**:
The product role Sinapso aims to fulfill by grounding exploration, research, synthesis, and decisions in the user's knowledge world.
_Avoid_: chatbot; it describes an interface pattern rather than the durable product relationship.
